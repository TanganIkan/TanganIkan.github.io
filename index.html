what is a mechanical kyboard

Mechanical keyboard refers to the mechanical system of how the keyboard works when the keys are pressed. This keyboard uses a mechanical

component design in the form of a switch that connects the keycap or keyboard keys with an electronic circuit underneath to send input to a PC and the output appears on the screen.
